Student Money-Saving Tips
1. Get Amazon Prime Student
6 months free, then 50% off. Free shipping saves you gas money and impulse trips to stores.
2. Buy used textbooks or rent
Check Chegg, Amazon, or your campus bookstore. Can save 50-70% per book.
3. Use student discounts everywhere
Spotify + Hulu bundle ($5.99), Apple Music ($5.99), many restaurants offer 10-15% off with student ID.
4. Meal prep on Sundays
Cook in bulk for the week. A $30 grocery haul can give you 10+ meals vs $10 per meal eating out.
5. Download budget tracking apps
Mint, YNAB (free for students), or PocketGuard help you see where money actually goes.
